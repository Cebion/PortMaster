## Controls

| Button | Action |
|--|--| 
|Select|Quit|
|Start|Quit|
|A|Open tile|
|B|Place/Remove flag|
|L1|Place/Remove flag|
|L2|Place/Remove flag|
|R2|Open tile|
|R1|Open tile|
|Left Analog|Move cursor|
|Right Analog|Move cursor|
