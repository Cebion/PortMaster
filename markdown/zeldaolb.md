## Controls

| Button | Action |
|--|--| 
|Start |Esc/Back/Pause|
|A|Attack (Z)|
|A|Interact|
|B|Attack (W)|
|X|Run|
|Y|Use selected object|
|L1|Pick an throw object|
|L2|See defetead monsters|
|L3|Maps|
|R1|Accept/menu|
|R2|Help|
|Up/Down/Left/Right|Movement|
